Datalox Env Data Proof v0 Handoff

Public-facing summary for collaborator review. Updated 2026-06-08.

What This Is

A 12-task MCP-backed domain-world seed for FlowCyto, Molecule Biology, and Protein MCP. It is designed to let a post-training collaborator inspect real tool trajectories and loader/eval shape before scaling data collection.

What Was Removed

Parser/report-only tasks, the toy sequence task, and preview lifecycle wrappers were removed from the active handoff.

Boundary

The verifier is a mechanical evidence gate, not a reward model. It checks captured tool_io:* evidence and simple workspace metadata. Zheng should decide the training objective, reward design, and rollout harness boundary.
